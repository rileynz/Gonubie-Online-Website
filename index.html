Honey, kept honest.

Gonubie Limited is a family-run apiary and export house on New Zealand's Coromandel coast. We produce, grade, and ship RILEY honey — mānuka, pōhutukawa, and native bush — to wholesalers, retailers, and distributors around the world.

Export & packaging

Packed for the distance

We ship to buyers across Asia, Europe, and the Middle East, with an export network built over years of customs clearance and freight partnerships. Honey leaves Whitianga in whatever format the buyer needs — bulk steel drums for processors, or finished jars ready for the shelf. Through an associated company in Australia, we can also supply Australian mānuka in a range of pack sizes.
