Graded & certified

The range

RILEY honey is harvested in the Coromandel Peninsula and graded before dispatch. Mānuka lines carry both an MGO figure (the measured methylglyoxal content) and a UMF rating (the certified overall potency standard) — the two figures buyers ask for most.

In development — 2027

RILEY Mead

Mead is honey wine — fermented from honey and water, and among the oldest fermented drinks on record. We're developing a RILEY mead range, from traditional sweet styles through to dry, sparkling, and low-alcohol versions, using old-world technique alongside our own honey. Release is intended for early 2027.
